This project analyzes skincare product prices in the market to uncover pricing patterns and customer behavior. It compares median prices across different skin types and product categories, highlighting how brands position themselves for different target segments.

A quick survey was also included to understand customer opinions on product pricing and influencer impact on purchasing decisions. Results were integrated into the dashboard as clear percentages and key insights.

The dashboard includes filters for skin type, gender, and age to enable deeper analysis.
