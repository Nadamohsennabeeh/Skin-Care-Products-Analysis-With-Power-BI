This project analyzes skincare product prices to understand market trends and customer behavior. It shows the median pricing for each skin type and compares prices across different product categories and brands to identify target segments.

It also includes survey insights about whether higher prices mean better quality and how influencers affect buying decisions. Results are presented through clear visuals and key insights.

Filters like skin type, gender, and age allow users to explore the data more easily.
